# Student Matching Application Requirements

## Project Overview

A university student-focused application designed to create an organized environment for knowledge exchange and academic support between students. The platform allows students to find suitable tutors or study partners even if they are outside their social circle.

---

# Project Goals

## Main Problem

There is currently no organized way for university students to exchange knowledge and academic support.
At the moment, help usually happens only between people who already know each other.

## Main Objective

Create a platform that helps students:

* Find tutors or study partners easily.
* Organize study appointments.
* Build a collaborative learning community.
* Create an environment where students feel safe and supported.

## Success Criteria

* Students actively using the application.
* Increased collaboration between students.
* Easier access to academic explanations and support.
* Building friendly relationships based on knowledge sharing.

---

# Value Provided by the Application

## For Students Seeking Help

* Easily finding the right tutor.
* Faster access to academic explanations.
* Saving time and effort.
* Ability to request help from students they do not personally know.

## For Student Tutors

* Ability to earn money.
* Ability to publish available tutoring times.
* Ability to connect with students who need help.

## Payment System

At the current stage, payment functionality will only be simulated for testing purposes.

---

# Target Audience

* University students.
* Students seeking academic help.
* Students capable of tutoring or teaching others.

---

# Student Information

When registering or creating an account, students should be able to provide:

* Basic personal information.
* Academic major.
* Academic grades or study level.

---

# Core Workflow

## Student Seeking Help

The student can:

* Browse available student tutors.
* Send appointment requests to tutors.
* Publish tutoring requests to find someone who can help.

## Student Tutor

The tutor can:

* Publish available times during the week.
* Search for students who need help.
* Send tutoring requests to students.

---

# Appointment System

## Available Appointments

* There are no general appointments available for all students.
* Only student tutors can define their available weekly time slots.

## Appointment Creation Process

* A request is sent by one of the two parties.
* The other party reviews and approves the request.
* After approval, the appointment becomes confirmed.

## Cancellation Rules

* Students can cancel appointments.
* Requests should automatically expire if not confirmed at least one hour before the appointment.
* Appointment cancellation is not allowed within the last 4 hours before the session starts.

## Appointment Conflict Handling

The system should use a reliable mechanism to prevent scheduling conflicts or double-booking.

---

# Attendance and Absence Handling

## If the Student Does Not Attend

* The session is treated as completed.
* The tutor receives the payment.

## If the Tutor Does Not Attend

* The student should be compensated.
* The tutor should lose the payment.

## Session Completion Confirmation

After the session ends, both participants should confirm through the application that the session has been completed.

---

# Academic Subjects

The platform supports all academic subjects.

---

# Session History

The application should maintain a history section containing:

* Previous sessions.
* Past appointments.
* Students the user interacted with.

---

# Trust and Safety

## Desired User Experience

Students should feel:

* Safe.
* Supported.
* Comfortable communicating with others.

## Information Verification

At the current stage, there is no process for verifying the accuracy of student information.

## Reporting Misconduct

Students should be able to report inappropriate behavior or misconduct.
